European Bank Regulatory Data: The EBA Disclosures Panel (2012–2025)

Eric Cuijpers, Maurice Bun · Zenodo · 2025

Zenodo

Overview

An analysis-ready panel of European bank regulatory disclosures compiled from all EBA Transparency Exercise releases since 2014, extended back to 2013 using the EBA stress test dataset. Coverage spans large banks in Europe, with semi-annual observations in early vintages and quarterly observations from 2018 onward.

Contents

  • Capital and leverage ratios
  • Balance sheet and P&L items
  • Risk-weighted asset (RWA) components
  • Detailed credit risk exposure breakdowns by portfolio and geography

Format

Provided in long format as CSV and Parquet files with a data dictionary.
